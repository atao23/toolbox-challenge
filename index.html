Memory Game

By: Annie Tao

Don't know how to play?

Instructions:

This is a simple game of memorization.

  1. Click on a tile to flip it over.
  2. Attempt to find its matching pair.
  3. If it's matching, congrats! You found a pair and the tiles will remain displayed. If not, try again.
  4. Once all the tiles have been flipped over, you've won the game!

After you've won, reset to beat your previous time. Have fun!
